Why PhytoSynergyDB

Curated synergy data,
one searchable database.

Antimicrobial resistance is one of the greatest threats to global health. The ESKAPE pathogens - Enterococcus faecium, Staphylococcus aureus, Klebsiella pneumoniae, Acinetobacter baumannii, Pseudomonas aeruginosa, and Enterobacter spp. - drive most hospital-acquired infections worldwide. PhytoSynergyDB catalogues plant-derived adjuvants that restore or amplify antibiotic activity against them.
