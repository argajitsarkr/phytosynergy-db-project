The pharmaceutical knowledgebase engineered to power your next discovery

Antimicrobial resistance (AMR) is one of the greatest threats to global health. The ESKAPE pathogens - Enterococcus faecium, Staphylococcus aureus, Klebsiella pneumoniae, Acinetobacter baumannii, Pseudomonas aeruginosa, and Enterobacter spp. - are responsible for the majority of hospital-acquired infections worldwide.

PhytoSynergyDB is the first curated database dedicated to cataloguing synergistic interactions between phytochemicals and conventional antibiotics against these critical pathogens. Each entry includes MIC values, FIC indices, synergy interpretations, and observed mechanisms of action.
