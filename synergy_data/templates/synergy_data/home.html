What is the FIC Index?

The Fractional Inhibitory Concentration (FIC) Index is the gold-standard metric for quantifying drug-combination interactions. It is calculated as:

FIC = (MICA in combo / MICA alone) + (MICB in combo / MICB alone)

All entries in PhytoSynergyDB include FIC values where available, extracted directly from the source publication.
