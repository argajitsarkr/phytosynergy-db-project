About PhytoSynergyDB

A collaborative, manually curated database for AMR researchers.

Our Mission

PhytoSynergyDB was created to address a critical gap in AMR research: the absence of a centralised, curated repository for phytochemical–antibiotic synergy data against clinically significant ESKAPE pathogens.

By systematically extracting and standardising Fractional Inhibitory Concentration (FIC) data from peer-reviewed literature published between 2020 and 2025, we aim to accelerate hypothesis generation, support drug discovery pipelines, and reduce duplication of effort across research groups worldwide.

What We Cover

  • Pathogens: All six ESKAPE species — Enterococcus faecium, Staphylococcus aureus, Klebsiella pneumoniae, Acinetobacter baumannii, Pseudomonas aeruginosa, and Enterobacter spp.
  • Phytochemicals: Plant-derived compounds including polyphenols, alkaloids, terpenoids, flavonoids, and essential oil constituents.
  • Antibiotics: All major antibiotic classes including beta-lactams, aminoglycosides, fluoroquinolones, macrolides, tetracyclines, and more.
  • FIC Data: MIC values tested alone and in combination, FIC Index, and FIC interpretation (Synergy / Additive / Indifference / Antagonism).
  • Mechanism of Action: Curated notes on the observed mechanisms of synergy where reported by the original authors.
  • Source Traceability: Every entry is linked to its source publication via DOI.

Curation Methodology

Literature was identified through systematic searches of PubMed, Web of Science, and Scopus using controlled vocabulary terms for ESKAPE pathogens, phytochemicals, antibiotic combinations, and FIC/MIC assays. Publications were included if they reported quantitative MIC data from standardised broth microdilution or agar dilution assays.

Data were extracted manually by trained curators and cross-validated against the source tables and figures. Compound identifiers were harmonised against PubChem CIDs where available.

How to Cite

If you use PhytoSynergyDB in your research, please cite:

PhytoSynergyDB: A curated database of phytochemical-antibiotic synergy against ESKAPE pathogens. [Manuscript in preparation]

Please also consider citing the individual source publications listed in each database entry.

Contact & Feedback

We welcome reports of errors, suggestions for additional data, and general feedback. If you have identified a publication that should be included in PhytoSynergyDB, please get in touch.

To report an issue or request a data addition, please open an issue on our project repository or contact the curation team directly.
