About PhytoSynergyDB

The first curated database dedicated to phytochemical-antibiotic synergies against ESKAPE pathogens.

The Problem

Antimicrobial resistance (AMR) is a growing global health crisis. The ESKAPE pathogens — Enterococcus faecium, Staphylococcus aureus, Klebsiella pneumoniae, Acinetobacter baumannii, Pseudomonas aeruginosa, and Enterobacter spp. — are responsible for the majority of hospital-acquired infections and are increasingly resistant to conventional antibiotics.

Our Approach

PhytoSynergyDB systematically curates data from peer-reviewed scientific literature on synergistic interactions between plant-derived phytochemicals and conventional antibiotics. Each entry includes Minimum Inhibitory Concentration (MIC) values, Fractional Inhibitory Concentration (FIC) indices, synergy interpretations, and observed mechanisms of action.

Data Standards

All data in PhytoSynergyDB is manually extracted from peer-reviewed publications and undergoes quality validation. Each record links to its original source via DOI, ensuring full traceability and reproducibility.

Contact

For questions, collaborations, or data contributions, please reach out to the PhytoSynergyDB team.
